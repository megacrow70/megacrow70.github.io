Recipe 2: Pancakes

This is the recipe for pancakes. It's a classic breakfast dish made with flour, eggs, milk, and butter.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Sugar

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together eggs and milk.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
  4. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  5. Pour batter into the skillet to form pancakes.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.

Details About My Artwork

While my artwork is inspired by the animation style from the early 2000s, I make it original especially by coming up with my own characters and my own titles for it, such as The Animatrix, which was loosely inspired by the Ben 10 franchise. However, instead of the ability to turn the user into different aliens, the titular device gives its user the ability to turn into different species of animals. In addition, the user of the animatrix can use the powers and abilities of the animals he transforms into to help people and defeat villains.

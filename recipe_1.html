Recipe 1: Fettucine Alfredo

This is the recipe for Fettucine Alfredo. It's a classic Italian dish made with fettuccine pasta and a creamy sauce.

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Heavy cream
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package directions.
  2. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Pour in heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
  5. Stir in grated Parmesan cheese until melted.
  6. Drain the cooked pasta and add it to the sauce.
